Research Site B

Garland Works!

So for the second research site I selected the database page called Garlandtools.org. I selected this page for a handful of reasons. The first one is that I find the page to be aesthetically pleasing to look at. The UI is also really user friendly and easy to navigate. A player can search up an item from the game and it will pop up on a list. If you click on the item, a small window will pop up in a separate section that shows alot of vital information. Some things that the page shoes are, how to obtain the item, whether its something that a player can create, or if it is obtained from hunting monsters, the currency required etc. Without getting too technical, it does a very good job of displaying information about an item and its uses. This ease of access makes Garlandtools one of my preferred sites.

Another cool feature that Garland tools has is the alarm feature and also the ability to open a table with items that are upgrades or improvements that are close to your character level. This can help someone plan out their upgrade choices as they level up.
